Individual Membership

Show the love. Join the jQuery Foundation on an annual subscription basis. You can choose to pay dues every 3 months (quarterly) or once per year (annual). To say thank you for your support, we'll send you some gifts each year depending on your membership level. See below for the other benefits you get for joining.

2013 Annual Thank You Gifts

  • jQuery Logo T-Shirt by American Apparel. Men's sizes: S-XXXL. Women's sizes: S-XXL
  • jQuery Logo Eco-Heather Zip Hoodie by Alternative Apparel. Unisex sizes: S-XXL.
  • jQuery Logo Laptop Commuter Bag by Timbuk2. Sizes: Small, Medium.
    (Note: Small will fit up to a 13 inch laptop. Medium is good for 15 to 17 inch laptops)

Additional Benefits

  • A membership card
  • Stickers and buttons
  • Recognition on the members page: jquery.org/members
  • A banner or badge you can put on your website or your laptop. Or both.
  • Discounts from companies that have joined the jQuery Foundation
  • Invites to special members-only parties at jQuery Foundation events

        Corporate Membership

        Your company can benefit greatly from membership in the jQuery Foundation. Benefits for companies include recognition on jQuery websites, recognition at jQuery conferences world-wide, and discounts on conference sponsorship packages. Also, when a company joins, we include individual membership for a few employees. For more info, contact membership@jquery.org.
